We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address when you create an account with email and password, or authentication data when you use Google or Apple sign-in.
  • Profile Photos: If you choose to add them to your player profiles. These are stored locally on your device only and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • App Settings: Your preferences for game settings and other app configurations, stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: Information about your subscription status or one-time purchases, processed and stored by Apple App Store or Google Play.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App and its functionality.
  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you when you sign in.
  • Save your preferences and settings locally on your device.
  • Enable player profile creation for games, stored locally on your device.
  • Process your subscription or purchase transactions.
  • Respond to your requests and support needs.

Data Storage and Security

Your account authentication information is stored securely using Firebase Authentication. Player profiles, photos, and app settings are stored locally on your device only. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate our App (such as Firebase for authentication).
  • If required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or legal process.
  • When we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or safety.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can access your account information at any time through the App.
  • Correction: You can update your profile information in the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account directly in the App, which will remove your authentication data from our systems.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data held by us by contacting us.

Account Deletion and Data Handling

  • Your authentication information will be permanently deleted from Firebase Authentication.
  • Your player profiles and photos are stored only on your device and can be deleted by you at any time.
  • Transaction records for purchases or subscriptions may be retained by Apple or Google in accordance with their privacy policies.
  • Some information may remain in our backup systems for a short period.

Children's Privacy

If you are a parent or guardian and believe we may have collected information from your child, please contact us so we can promptly remove the information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a player profile?
A: Go to the Players section in the app and tap the + button to create a new player profile.

Q: How does the subscription work?
A: Our subscription provides full access to all games and features. You can choose between a monthly subscription or a 24-hour access pass.

Q: Can I use the app offline?
A: Yes! Once you've logged in, the games can be played without an internet connection.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Subscriptions can be managed through your App Store or Google Play account settings.
