Delete Account & Data

Last Updated: March 26, 2025

How to Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open PineOrange.
  2. Tap the three-line drawer button in the bottom-left corner.
  3. Select My Account.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. On the prompt “All your data will be permanently deleted. This cannot be undone.”, tap Delete.
  6. If you have not logged in recently, a snackbar message will ask you to log out and sign back in before repeating the steps above—this is a safety measure.
  7. After deletion is complete, you will be automatically signed out.

What Gets Deleted

When you confirm deletion, the following data is removed permanently:

  • Firebase Authentication record (your UID, email address, display name, and linked providers such as Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In)
  • Firestore user documents (Agreements and any other documents under your UID)
  • RevenueCat custom subscriber attributes

Any server backups that already include your data will be purged automatically within 30 days.

What Does Not Get Deleted

  • RevenueCat purchase history and entitlements. Your App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) purchases remain intact. To restore them on the same device tied to your platform account, open PineOrange and tap Restore Purchases.
  • Active subscriptions in your device’s subscription settings. You must cancel these manually via your Apple ID subscriptions (iOS Settings → Subscriptions) or Google Play Store account to stop future billing.
  • Locally cached app data (any temporary or offline data created on your device). This will remain until you uninstall the app from your device.

Any server backups that already include your data will be purged automatically within 30 days.

What Does Not Get Deleted Automatically

  • Your active subscription in the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. You must cancel it manually in your device’s subscription settings to stop future billing.
  • Payment transaction records that Apple or Google are required to keep for tax and anti-fraud purposes.
